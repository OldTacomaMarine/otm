Engine History

The ferry Skansonia is powered by two Fairbanks-Morse 35F10 (6) diesel engines, installed when the ferry was built in 1928. The Skansonia is now a stationary events venue, and the engine room is virtually untouched since the vessel's 1970s decommissioning.
