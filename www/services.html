Old Tacoma Marine, Inc.

Services Provided by OTM Inc.

Old Tacoma Marine Inc specializes in large-bore, slow-speed, antique diesel engines produced before 1955, especially those manufactured by the Atlas-Imperial, Fairbanks-Morse, Enterprise Foundry, and Washington Iron Works companies. Old Tacoma Marine Inc. also works with old-style Cooper-Bessemer, Winton, Kahlenberg, Sulzer, and other engines.
