Enterprise Foundry Co

Known remaining Enterprise diesel engines

Enterprise model numbers are a combination of three different letter designations: "D" (diesel), "M" (marine), or "S" (stationary), and a designation that indicates the cylinder size (M, W, G, Q, etc). These three letter designations are followed by the number of cylinders in the model. For example, a DMQ-8 is a marine diesel engine with a 16" bore, 20" stroke, and eight cylinders.
