Engine History

The Western New York Gas & Steam Show Association has a three-cylinder Y model Fairbanks-Morse on display at their grounds in Alexander, NY. The engine was one of two Fairbanks removed from the Factory Power House in Nunda, New York, and restored by the Assocation.
